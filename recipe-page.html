Creamy Pasta Recipe

A simple, quick and delicious pasta.

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup cheese
  • 1 clove garlic (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp black pepper
  • Salt as needed

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until soft, then drain.
  2. In a pan, melt butter and add garlic.
  3. Pour in milk and stir gently.
  4. Add cheese, salt, and black pepper.
  5. Mix pasta into the sauce and cook for 2â€“3 minutes.
  6. Serve hot with extra cheese on top.

Enjoy your creamy pasta!
